About Us



AS CivEng Design is immersed in all phases of cell site design development and utilize the latest technology, along with a common sense approach, to provide the best solutions for our clients. We perform all aspects of design, structural and civil engineering, undertaken by our internal team of Design Engineers, Project Managers, Design Coordinators & CAD technicians. If we say briefly what defines us would be:

Quality:



Our team uses advanced custom software programs to model and analyse the roads, platforms, billboards, metal structures (rooftops or telecommunication towers), to determine the ability of the structures to withstand additional loads, as well as to identify the need for reinforcement.

Short delivery time of works and seriousness:



Our clients rely on us for innovative and cost-effective solutions for our projects, for the seriousness and promptness we show, as well as for the short response time and delivery of works.

Competitive prices on the market:



We pride ourselves on offering a quality service at market competitive prices. With a combination of vast experience within our team and a ‘can do’ attitude and ethos, allows us to maximise each work package and  deliver good value for our clients.
